Everything You Need to Build Cards

Formation Library

20+ pre-built offensive formations ready to go. Doubles, Trips, Empty, Pistol, I-Form, Bunch, Unbalanced — click and start building.

One-Click Routes

Click any player, pick a route. Verticals, intermediates, quick game, runs, blocks, motions — all organized by category. No freehand drawing needed.

Defensive Front Overlay

Drop your defense on top of the offensive look. 4-3 Over, Under, Tite, Odd, Bear — see both sides of the ball on one card.

Export to PDF & PNG

Print-ready scout cards in one click. Export your full card deck and hand it to your scout team before practice.

Freehand Drawing

Need to mark up something custom? Freehand pen, shapes, text boxes, multiple colors and line widths — all built in.

Save Presets

Save your most-used formations, defensive fronts, and route combos as presets. Reuse them week after week all season.
